The Brief

The brief was to come up with branding, identity and website design that was premium, fresh, vibrant and a modern aesthetic that is engaging and visually stimulating. After getting the broad outline of the vision, I set about curating the various design elements that would bring the vision to life.

The Approach

Cohesion. The aim was to merge various design elements and ideas into a cohesive design narative that visually stimulates the target audience while telling the brand’s story effectively.

Logo:The logo combines modern text with a colourful bookmark tag. I broke down the logo into three parts: Part 1 is the reverse html elements, they’re reversed to imply willingness to break away from convention. Part 2 has the brand initials in display font arranged to have a forward pointing arrow between them, this implies a brand ethos of being modern and yet still forward thinking. Lastly, Part 3 is the colourful gradient bookmark tag that is the main part of the logo. It’s vibrant and it’s memorable.

Colour Scheme:The brand vision and identify had to be communicated clearly through the colours and to achieve this I picked colours that are complimentary of each other and representative of what we wanted to achieve. The main colour is black: premium, strong, elegant and sophisticated. The secondary colour is red-orange: vibrant, creative, enthusiastic and stimulating. I rounded off the colour scheme with white as the accent colour.

Typography: I settled on a combination of a display and sans serif font for the brand typography. The display font, Neuropol is the main brand font. It is unique, modern with loads of personality. The small text font is Poiret One, it is clean, forward-thinking and honest.

Website Design

I built this website from scratch mainly with HTML & CSS. I decided that user experience should be the common string that ties the website together design wise. The information needed to be easy to access and concise enough not to overwhelm.

The End Product

Working on this gave me tremendous pride and a deep sense of fulfilment. This was personal for me and I hope this sentiment shines through for all that navigate the site.
