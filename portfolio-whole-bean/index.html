The Whole Bean

Branding & Identity Design
Oct 2019

The Whole Bean is a neighbourhood
coffee shop. I came up with a branding
& identity design concept for their
launch.

The Brief

Subsequent to a sit-down with the client and factoring in the target audience/market, it was clear that what the client wanted was a fresh, sincere, welcoming and elegant brand aesthetic that gave you a feel of being at home. I experimented with quite a few ideas of what logo would best capture the elements in the client’s brief.

The Approach

Design at its best is collaborative and as such my approach is to always ensure that the client’s voice is heard through the logo, colour scheme, typography and imagery curated to achieve the finished product.

Logo:The logo is a combination shapes that allows you to view the coffee cup from 2 angles, aerially and from the side. From the side you see a cup of coffee and a line under it representing the saucer. While aerially you see the cup with the full view of the saucer and a stirrer. It’s fresh, it’s simple and it’s memorable.

Colour Scheme:I picked colours that are complimentary and representative of the brand feel and identity. The main colour is brown: welcoming, warm, cosy and relaxing. The secondary colour is turquoise: renewal, expressiveness, wholeness and tranquillity. I rounded off the colour scheme with shades of grey as the accent colour.

Typography:I settled on a combination of a script and sans serif font for the brand typography. The script font, Al Fresco is the main brand font. It is elegant, creative and personal with an eye towards earning customer affinity. The small text font is Rogue Sans, it is clean, modern and engaging.

Imagery:Imagery conveys warmth, cosiness and welcome. From the people to the offerings, the images and their tone was carefully curated to make sure they stayed on brand and reinforced the brand identity and aesthetics.

The End Product

Like I stated earlier, design is collaborative. This client was a joy to collaborate with, we bounced ideas off each other and the finished product is something everyone is happy with and very proud of.
