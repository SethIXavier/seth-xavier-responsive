Aina & Sanmi

Wedding Website Design
Sept 2019

The couple were a joy to work with. I
designed their wedding website &
wedding notifications

The Brief

The lovely couple wanted a clean, minimalist aesthetic that reflected their personal styles for their wedding web site. I employed muted colours and used white space to achieve their vision.

The Approach

Simplicity. Above all else the couple wanted the style to be simple and the website to be shallow and easy to navigate.

Colour Scheme:The dominant colour we decided on is powder pink: affection, harmony and universal love. I completed the colour scheme by using different shades of grey to black as the accent colour.

Typography:Being a wedding brief I just had to use an expressive script font. For this brief that font was Volina. The aim was to convey elegance. The small text fonts are Briller & Galahad-std, to convey minimalism.

Imagery:I picked one hero image for the website that best expresses what the couple felt and represents them accurately.

Website Design

The couple and I had sat down and agreed on what the website should look like and how easy they wanted the usability to be. Armed with this I went ahead to come up with a sitemap, concept and wireframes. These were shown to the couple for the final all clear then I went ahead and built the website.

The End Product

It was great fun being a tiny part of this love story. The couple basically gave me broad outlines of what they wanted and trusted me to make them happy. They were happy, so was I!
