About

Odutola Odunaiya is a Web Development and Visual Design consultant. He works under the name Seth Xavier.

Odutola broke into tech on the back of 15 years of varied experience spanning events to operations for governmental entities and private sectors companies in Africa and the Middle East.
A customer-centric professional, Odutola now helps small businesses, start-ups and individuals to maximise their online presence as well as offering conceptualisation and design services for branding & identity planning.
When Odutola is not working, he is passionate about design (in all forms), travel and Arsenal FC. Utopia would be being able to do all three at the same time. Because that great idea can come to you at the oddest of times, Odutola can be reached at odutola@seth-xavier.com or alternatively, pop over to our contact page and fill out our form.
